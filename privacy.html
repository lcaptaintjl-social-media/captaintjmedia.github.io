Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Captain TJ Media ("the Application", "we", "us") handles information in connection with the use of this automated social media publishing tool.

1. About the Application

Captain TJ Media is a personal automation application used exclusively by the owner of the Captain TJ YouTube channel to post video content to connected social media accounts, including TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook. This application is not a public service and is not available for registration or use by third parties.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

This application does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal data belonging to third parties. Specifically:

  • No user registration or account creation is required by any third party
  • No personal data (names, email addresses, phone numbers) of third parties is collected
  • No analytics, tracking, or behavioral data is gathered
  • No cookies or persistent tracking technologies are used

3. OAuth Access Tokens

To enable automated posting, the Application uses OAuth 2.0 to authorize access to social media accounts owned by the channel operator. OAuth access tokens are:

  • Stored locally within the automation infrastructure controlled by the channel owner
  • Never transmitted to or stored by any third party other than the originating platform (e.g., TikTok, Meta)
  • Used solely to post content to accounts owned and operated by the channel owner
  • Revocable at any time through the respective platform's account settings

4. Content and Data

The Application processes video files and metadata (titles, descriptions, captions) provided by the channel owner. This content is transmitted directly to the destination social media platforms via their official APIs. No content is stored by the Application beyond what is needed for temporary processing.

5. Third-Party Platforms

This Application interacts with the following third-party platforms via their official APIs:

  • TikTok (TikTok Content Posting API)
  • Meta (Instagram Graph API, Facebook Pages API)
  • YouTube (YouTube Data API v3)

Each platform's own privacy policy governs how they handle data submitted through their APIs. The Application does not control how these platforms process or store submitted content.

6. Data Security

OAuth credentials and access tokens are stored within a self-hosted automation environment controlled exclusively by the channel owner. No cloud-based credential storage is used. Access is restricted to the channel owner only.

7. Children's Privacy

This Application is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
